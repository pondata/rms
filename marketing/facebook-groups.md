# Facebook Groups to Join
Search for and join these. Don't pitch — be helpful, answer questions, have your business page linked in your profile.

## Groups to search for:
- Vancouver WA Small Business Network
- Clark County Small Business Owners
- Vancouver WA Business Networking
- Portland Small Business Owners
- PDX Entrepreneurs
- SW Washington Business Network
- Vancouver WA Community
- Battle Ground / Ridgefield / Camas business groups

## First post template (after being in the group for a few days):

"Hey everyone! I'm Justin, I run River Mountain Systems — a local IT services company here in Vancouver. We help small businesses with tech support, cybersecurity, cloud setup, and network infrastructure.

I see a lot of tech questions in groups like this, so I'll be hanging around to help where I can. If you ever have an IT question, feel free to tag me or shoot me a message. No sales pitch — just happy to help local businesses.

Website if you're curious: rivermountainsystems.com"

## Ongoing strategy:
- Check groups daily for tech questions
- Answer helpfully without pitching
- Share your blog articles when relevant (not more than 1x/week)
- Comment on other people's posts to build visibility
