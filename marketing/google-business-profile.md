# Google Business Profile Setup
Go to business.google.com → "Manage now"

## Step by step:

1. Business name: **River Mountain Systems**

2. Business category: **Computer support and services**
   - Add additional: IT service provider, Computer security service

3. Do you want to add a location? 
   - If you have an office: add the address
   - If you work from home: select "I deliver goods and services to my customers" and hide your address

4. Service area: 
   - Vancouver, WA
   - Portland, OR
   - Clark County, WA
   - Camas, WA
   - Washougal, WA
   - Battle Ground, WA
   - Ridgefield, WA
   - Beaverton, OR
   - Hillsboro, OR

5. Contact:
   - Phone: (360) 644-4820
   - Website: https://rivermountainsystems.com

6. Hours: Mon-Fri 8am-6pm (or whatever you want)

7. Description (750 char max):
River Mountain Systems provides managed IT services for small and mid-size businesses in Vancouver, WA and the Portland metro area. We handle IT support, cybersecurity, cloud migrations, network infrastructure, backup and disaster recovery, and IT consulting. Our proactive approach prevents problems before they cause downtime. Flat-rate monthly pricing with no surprise invoices. Free initial consultation. Call (360) 644-4820 or visit rivermountainsystems.com.

8. Add photos:
   - Use your business card image
   - Screenshot of your website
   - Any photos of you working / at a client site

9. After setup, go to Posts and create your first post:
   - Title: Free IT Assessment for Local Businesses
   - Body: Not sure if your IT setup is helping or hurting your business? River Mountain Systems offers a free, no-obligation IT assessment for small businesses in the Vancouver/Portland area. We'll review your network, security, and backups and give you an honest evaluation. Book yours at rivermountainsystems.com or call (360) 644-4820.
   - Button: Learn more → https://rivermountainsystems.com/#contact

## After verification:

10. Go to Google Search Console (search.google.com/search-console)
    - Add property → URL prefix → https://rivermountainsystems.com
    - Verify (they'll give you a method — DNS TXT record is easiest)
    - Go to Sitemaps → Add → sitemap.xml
