# Google Review Outreach — River Mountain Systems
**Goal:** 25 reviews in 90 days. Start within 48 hours of GBP verification.

---

## Before You Send Anything

1. **Get your GBP review link:**
   - Go to business.google.com → Your profile → "Ask for reviews"
   - Copy the short link (looks like `g.page/r/...`)
   - Drop it everywhere below

2. **Prioritize your list** — rank contacts by:
   - Likelihood to respond (people who already like you)
   - Recency of interaction (helped them in last 12 months)
   - Relationship warmth (friend > acquaintance > cold contact)

3. **Send in batches of 5–10** — Google flags profiles that get 20 reviews in a day

---
