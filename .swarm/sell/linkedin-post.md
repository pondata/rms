## What to include / not include

- **Include:** the price, the wedge (posted + SLA + pilot), one phone number, the URL.
- **Skip:** logos you don't have, certifications you can't prove, "trusted by" claims.
- **Hashtags:** 1–3 max. Recommended: `#VancouverWA #ManagedIT #SmallBusiness`. Local hashtags get more weight than generic.
- **Image:** post WITH the headshot at `/screenshots/justin-headshot.jpg`. Image posts get ~30% more reach than text-only.
- **Link:** drop `rivermountainsystems.com` as the FIRST COMMENT, not in the body. LinkedIn deboosts posts with external links in the body.
- **Don't post and ghost.** Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours — that's the engagement signal.
