Network Design & Structured Cabling

A reliable network starts with proper design. We plan network architecture around your actual needs — number of users, bandwidth requirements, VoIP traffic, cloud application usage, and growth projections — then build it with structured cabling that lasts decades, not just until the next office rearrangement.

  • Site surveys and heat mapping to determine optimal switch and access point placement
  • Cat6/Cat6A structured cabling with proper termination, labeling, and documentation
  • VLAN segmentation to isolate guest traffic, IoT devices, and sensitive systems
  • Rack and patch panel organization that makes future troubleshooting straightforward
  • Network diagrams and documentation so any technician can understand your setup
10G

Future-ready cabling

We install Cat6A cabling that supports 10 Gigabit speeds — even if you do not need that today. Pulling cable is the most disruptive part of a network build. Do it once, do it right, and you will not need to re-cable when your bandwidth needs grow.

Enterprise Wi-Fi

Consumer-grade routers and mesh systems are not built for business environments. We deploy enterprise access points with centralized management, proper authentication, and the capacity to handle dozens of concurrent users without degradation.

  • Wi-Fi 6/6E access points from vendors like Ubiquiti, Meraki, or Aruba — matched to your budget and scale
  • Predictive heat mapping and on-site surveys to eliminate dead zones and interference
  • Separate SSIDs for corporate devices, guest access, and IoT with isolated VLANs behind each
  • 802.1X authentication for corporate devices — no shared passwords that walk out the door with former employees
  • Centralized management for firmware updates, policy changes, and usage monitoring across all locations
0

Dead zones

We do not guess where to put access points. RF site surveys and heat mapping show exactly where coverage gaps exist, where interference is strongest, and where channel overlap is causing slowdowns. The result is consistent, fast Wi-Fi everywhere your team works.

Firewalls, VPNs & Secure Remote Access

Your firewall is the front door of your network. We deploy business-grade firewalls with intrusion detection, content filtering, and VPN capabilities — giving your team secure access from anywhere while keeping threats out.

  • Next-gen firewalls (Fortinet, SonicWall, Meraki MX) with unified threat management
  • Site-to-site VPN tunnels connecting branch offices with encrypted, low-latency links
  • Remote access VPN for employees working from home — with split tunneling options for performance
  • Content filtering and application control to block malicious sites and shadow IT
  • Geo-IP blocking and IDS/IPS to detect and stop inbound attack patterns
99.9%

Uptime target

We configure failover and redundancy into critical network components. Dual WAN connections, high-availability firewall pairs, and automated failover mean a single point of failure does not take your entire business offline.

Network Monitoring & Management

Once your network is built, it needs ongoing attention. We monitor bandwidth usage, device health, and security events continuously — catching issues like a failing switch port or a saturated uplink before your team notices slowdowns.

  • SNMP and flow-based monitoring of all switches, routers, access points, and firewalls
  • Automated alerts for link failures, high utilization, temperature warnings, and device reboots
  • Bandwidth reporting that shows who and what is consuming your internet connection
  • Regular firmware updates and configuration backups so you can recover from hardware failures quickly
  • Quarterly network reviews to plan for growth, new locations, or technology upgrades
24/7

Network visibility

We know the health of every device on your network at all times. When a switch port goes down at 2 AM, we know about it and can have a plan in place before your team arrives in the morning.
