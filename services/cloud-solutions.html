Microsoft 365 & Google Workspace

These platforms are more than email. When configured properly, they become the productivity backbone of your business — document collaboration, video conferencing, shared calendars, internal sites, and identity management all in one place.

We handle the full lifecycle:

  • Tenant setup with proper security defaults, MFA, and conditional access policies
  • Email migration from on-premises Exchange, IMAP, or competing platforms with zero data loss
  • SharePoint and OneDrive or Google Drive structure planning — so your files are organized from day one
  • Teams or Google Meet deployment with proper channels, permissions, and guest access controls
  • Ongoing license optimization — making sure you are not paying for Business Premium when Business Basic covers your needs
M365

The most common migration we do

Most small businesses are either underusing their Microsoft 365 subscription or overpaying for it. We audit your current license tier, enable the security features you are already paying for, and train your team on the tools that save time.

AWS & Azure Cloud Infrastructure

For businesses that need more than SaaS — line-of-business applications, database hosting, virtual desktops, or development environments — we design and manage cloud infrastructure on AWS or Azure.

We focus on right-sizing, not over-engineering:

  • Architecture design that matches your actual workload, not a vendor's upsell
  • Virtual server (EC2/Azure VM) deployment with proper networking, backups, and monitoring
  • Cost management through reserved instances, auto-scaling, and regular spend reviews
  • Hybrid cloud setups connecting your office network to cloud resources via secure VPN or ExpressRoute/Direct Connect
30%

Average cloud cost reduction

Many businesses overprovision when they first move to the cloud. We right-size your instances, eliminate idle resources, and implement reserved pricing — typically cutting monthly cloud bills by 20-40% without reducing performance.

Migration Planning & Execution

Cloud migrations fail when they are rushed. We follow a structured process that maps your current environment, identifies dependencies, and moves workloads in phases — so your team never loses a day of productivity.

  • Discovery and assessment of existing servers, applications, and data stores
  • Dependency mapping to identify which systems need to move together
  • Phased migration with rollback plans at every stage
  • Parallel running period where old and new systems operate side by side
  • Post-migration validation, performance testing, and user acceptance
0

Acceptable hours of unplanned downtime

We schedule migrations during off-hours, maintain rollback capabilities throughout, and validate every system before cutting over. Your team shows up Monday morning and everything works — that is the goal, every time.

Ongoing Cloud Management

Moving to the cloud is not the finish line — it is the starting point. Cloud environments need ongoing attention: security patches, cost optimization, performance tuning, and user support as your business grows.

  • Monthly cloud spend reviews with actionable recommendations
  • Security monitoring for unusual access patterns, exposed resources, and misconfigurations
  • Automated scaling policies that adjust resources based on actual demand
  • Regular backup verification and disaster recovery testing
